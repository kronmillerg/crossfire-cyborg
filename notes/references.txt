Local paths to some interesting game files (on Linux Mint 18.1 Serena; probably
the paths are the same on most/all Ubuntu- and Debian-based systems):
    /usr/share/games/crossfire/           -- top level dir
    /usr/share/games/crossfire/maps/      -- maps
    /usr/share/games/crossfire/archetypes -- item archetypes
    /usr/share/games/crossfire/formulae   -- alchemical formulae
